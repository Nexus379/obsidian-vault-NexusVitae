> [!info]- SRS Card Syntax
> **One-way card:** `Question :: Answer`
>
> **Reversed card:** `Question ::: Answer`
>
> **Cloze card:** `The capital of Iceland is ==Reykjavik==.`
>
> **Multi-line card:**
> ```text
> Question
> ?
> Answer line 1
> Answer line 2
> ```
>
> Use `#vocabcards` for cards reviewed by the Obsidian Spaced Repetition community plugin.
> Use `#studycards` for Nexus-internal Studycard notes with `space_lvl`, `space_rank`, and `space_date`.
