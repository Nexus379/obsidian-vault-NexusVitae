> [!info]- 📖 Spaced Repetition Community Plugin Syntax Cheat Sheet
> **1. Single-Line Card (Front & Back)**
> Question :: Answer
> 
> **2. Single-Line Reversed Card (Bi-directional)**
> Question ::: Answer
> 
> **3. Multi-Line Card (Top & Bottom)**
> Question ?
> Answer
> 
> **4. Cloze Deletion (Fill-in-the-blank)**
> Rome is the {capital} of {Italy}.
> *or numbered cloze:*
> Rome is the {c1::capital} of {c2::Italy}.
> 
> ---
> - **`::`**: Inline flashcard separator (Front :: Back).
> - **`:::`**: Two-way flashcard separator (creates front-to-back and back-to-front cards).
> - **`?`**: Multi-line flashcard separator (Question on top line, `?`, Answer below).
> - **`{...}`**: Cloze deletion for fill-in-the-blank memory tests.
